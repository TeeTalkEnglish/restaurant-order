Food Court Vocab

Useful language to recycle during the role-play

Eat in the food court or take the food away.
Combo
Meal plus chips or drink for one price.
Pickup number
Number called when food is ready.
Sauce on the side
Sauce separate, not mixed into the meal.
Mild / spicy
How hot the food tastes.
Surcharge
Small extra card payment fee.
