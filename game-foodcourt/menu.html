Useful food court phrases

  • "Can I get this as a combo, please?"
  • "Is it spicy? Can you make it mild?"
  • "Does this have nuts, dairy, or seafood in it?"
  • "Could I get the sauce on the side?"
  • "Is this for here or takeaway?" - "Takeaway, please."
  • "What pickup number am I?"
Pickup screens are near each stall. Listen for your number and keep your receipt.
Cards accepted · Some stalls add a small card surcharge.
