📚 Key Aussie Café Words to Remember
Brekkie 🍳
= Breakfast. "What do you want for brekkie?"
Flat White ☕
Most popular Aussie coffee. Espresso + steamed milk.
Long Black ☕
Strong black coffee. Like an Americano.
Avo 🥑
= Avocado. "Can I add avo to that?"
Chips 🍟
= Hot French fries. NOT the bag kind!
Toastie 🥪
= Toasted sandwich. "A cheese toastie, please."
Tap water 💧
FREE water from the tap. Always ask for it!
EFTPOS 💳
= Electronic card payment machine. "Can I tap?"
Schnitzel 🍗
Crumbed and fried meat. Very popular in Australia!
Lamington 🍰
Iconic Aussie cake. Chocolate + coconut sponge.
Vegemite 🍞
Dark salty spread. Very Australian! Try a little first.
Poached eggs 🥚
Eggs cooked in hot water. Very common on brekkie menus!
